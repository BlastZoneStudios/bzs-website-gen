About Us

Blast Zone Studios is an indie software development company founded in early 2013. The company specializes in creating mobile games for Android and iOS. Blast Zone Studios manages to produce enjoyable games remarkably quick. Titles including Sushi Bear and Hoops Up were created in less then a week. The company strives to create addicting games that are unique and accessible to people of all ages.
